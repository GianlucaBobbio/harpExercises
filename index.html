The Harmonica Scale Exercises Project

The Harmonica Scale Exercises is a project developed by and for harmonica players.
You just need to select the position and scale/mode you want to practice and then press the "Get random exercise" button.
I add the degrees so you can play over a 12 bar blues, or any other backing track, with the generated pattern.
Or you can just play around, up and down, through the exercise.

Right now you have above 15.000 exercises combinations! Go ahead, and enjoy the practice!

I will be updating the website and adding more exercises, positions, and scales, over the time. All rights reserved.
